COVID-19 Boosters for Lancaster General Health Employees

The LG Health Convenience Pharmacies offer several vaccines, by appointment, to employees, their dependents and patients seen by LG Health clinicians.

  • Lancaster General Health employees and dependents (ages 18 and older and at least 2 months out from last COVID vaccine or illness) can receive COVID-19 bivalent vaccine boosters, by appointment, from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. on Tuesdays and Fridays at the Convenience Pharmacy's Duke Street location on the first floor of Lancaster General Hospital. Both Pfizer and Moderna formulations of the vaccine are available.
  • For patients and dependents under 18, COVID-19 bivalent vaccine boosters are available through your primary care provider. Review the CDC guidelines for recommendations for children under age 18.

Learn more about these vaccines and additional vaccines that are available at the Convenience Pharmacy. To schedule an appointment, please call 717-544-5929.
