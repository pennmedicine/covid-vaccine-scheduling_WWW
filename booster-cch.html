Penn Medicine Employee COVID-19 Vaccine Booster Scheduling

COVID-19 Boosters for Chester County Hospital Employees

Bivalent COVID-19 vaccine boosters are now available and are designed to provide better protection against the Omicron variant. Bivalent COVID-19 boosters are not required for Chester County Hospital employees at this time. However, we do provide options for employees who would like to receive one.

COVID boosters will be offered from noon to 3 p.m., Mondays through Fridays in the Employee Resource Center (ERC). No appointment necessary. The ERC is located in Pre-Procedure Testing on the ground floor of Chester County Hospital's Pavilion, 701 East Marshall Street, West Chester, PA.

CCH will offer both Pfizer and Moderna formulations of the booster, as supplies allow.

Questions? Email InfectionPreventionCCH@pennmedicine.upenn.edu.
